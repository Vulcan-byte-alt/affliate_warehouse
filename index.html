Our Story

Built From 25 Years of
Trade Relationships

Affiliate Warehouse didn't come from a boardroom. It came from understanding a structural problem that affects every preferred contractor in Australia.

The Origin

Affiliate Warehouse was born alongside its sister company, Aussie Antennas and Electrical — a national electrical services business that has worked with trusted contractors across Australia for more than 25 years.

The Model That Worked

Aussie Antennas and Electrical scaled nationally by building long-term relationships with their preferred contractors. The model worked. Jobs flowed. Contractors stayed busy. Clients were looked after.

The Problem We Found

But as business grew, a structural problem became clear. Every preferred contractor purchased materials through their own individual trade accounts — small-business pricing, inconsistent discounts, and layered mark-ups. Those costs were passed back through the system. National-scale purchasing was being handled like hundreds of separate small businesses.

The Better Question

So instead of squeezing contractors, renegotiating every invoice, or cutting corners, we stepped back and asked a better question: What if our preferred contractors purchased together?

The Answer

That question became Affiliate Warehouse. Built as a separate, independent business, Affiliate Warehouse exists to manage procurement properly — at scale. One shared purchasing system. Corporate-level supplier pricing. Used by preferred contractors nationwide. Same contractors. Same independence. Just better pricing for everyone involved.

What wholesaler accounts do I get access to?
Affiliate Warehouse currently operates with TradeZone, Ideal Electrical, and Voltex. Access is through Affiliate Warehouse's national corporate accounts — and all purchases remain processed through standard wholesaler channels, fully traceable and job-coded.
How does Affiliate Warehouse make money?
Only when you save. Affiliate Warehouse retains 1.1% of the verified discount achieved on a purchase. If no discount is achieved on an order — there is no fee for that order, or any other. There are no subscription fees, setup costs, or monthly minimums.
Am I locked in to using Affiliate Warehouse?
No — and this is deliberate. There are no contracts, no minimum spend requirements, and no exclusivity obligations. You can continue using your existing trade accounts alongside Affiliate Warehouse, and you can stop participating at any time with no process or penalty.
How do I know if pricing is actually improving?
You can compare Affiliate Warehouse pricing against your original trade account rates at any time. The platform tracks pricing on your repeat purchases over time, giving you a clear, direct comparison. Optional email alerts also notify you when prices on your regular items move up or down.
Is this compliant with supplier and wholesaler systems?
Yes. Affiliate Warehouse operates within existing wholesaler systems and terms. All purchases are job-coded, fully traceable, and processed through normal wholesale channels. There is no reselling, no bypassing of supplier terms, and no alteration of wholesaler pricing structures. Participation is voluntary and non-exclusive.
What if Affiliate Warehouse stops being useful for my business?
Simply stop participating. There is no cancellation process, no exit penalties, and no questions asked. Affiliate Warehouse is designed to remain useful — not to retain you through obligation or friction.
How are better prices achieved?
Through aggregated purchasing volume. Independent preferred contractors choosing to purchase collectively through national corporate accounts may qualify for pricing tiers typically available to larger volume operators — subject to wholesaler approval and prevailing terms. Volume and purchasing consistency are the primary drivers.
Can I still use my existing trade accounts?
Absolutely. Participation is completely non-exclusive. You are encouraged to compare Affiliate Warehouse pricing against your existing accounts at any time — and to use whichever delivers better value on each individual purchase.
How do I identify myself when making a purchase?
When making a purchase or pick-up, identify yourself as a preferred contractor for Affiliate Warehouse and quote your Affiliate Warehouse account number and purchase order number. All payments are made by credit or debit card — Affiliate Warehouse does not extend credit to preferred contractors. If a verified discount is achieved, Affiliate Warehouse retains up to 1.1% of that saving. You keep the rest.
